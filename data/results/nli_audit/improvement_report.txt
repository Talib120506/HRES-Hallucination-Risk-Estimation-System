[CAUSE-2] NLI Model Fooled by Hallucinated Answers
Evidence: entailment_score >= 0.90 for actual hallucinations.
Rows affected: 81 (68.1% of errors)
Explanation: The hallucination dataset uses highly deceptive or lexically overlapping phrases that the baseline NLI model incorrectly maps as 'Entailed'.

[CAUSE-3] High 'UNCERTAIN' Verdict Ambiguity
Evidence: NLI verdict fell into the 0.60–0.90 entailment gap.
Rows affected: 37 (31.1% of errors)
Explanation: The model lacks confidence, returning 'UNCERTAIN', which defaults to flagging the text as hallucinated (Label=1). If these were mostly correct answers (Label=0), the entailment threshold is too strict.

[CAUSE-4] Weak Retrieval for Specific Documents
Evidence: Disproportionate errors concentrated in a few PDFs.
Rows affected: 30 (25.2% of errors)
Problematic doc_ids: lg_home_theater.pdf, omron_monitor.pdf, tesla_model_s.pdf
Explanation: These specific PDFs may have poor text extraction, OCR issues, or complex chunking requirements that standard FAISS Euclidean distance is failing to map.
